Labotary Department (Hameeda Hospital)

Overview

    Department Structure

  • Clinical Chemistry: Analyzes blood and body fluids for chemical components, including glucose, enzymes, and electrolytes.
  • Hematology: Focuses on blood disorders and diseases, conducting tests like complete blood counts (CBC) and coagulation studies.
  • Microbiology: Identifies infectious agents like bacteria, viruses, fungi, and parasites through cultures and sensitivity testing.

    Facilities and Equipment

  • Automated Analyzers: High-tech machines for rapid and accurate testing of blood, urine, and other samples.
  • Microscopes: Advanced optical and electron microscopes for detailed examination of cells and tissues.
  • Sample Processing Area: Where specimens are prepared and processed for various tests.
