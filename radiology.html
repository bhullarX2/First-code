Radiology Department (Hameeda Hospital)

Overview

    Department Structure

  • Diagnostic Radiology: Provides imaging services to diagnose various medical conditions using techniques like X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs.
  • Interventional Radiology: Performs minimally invasive procedures guided by imaging, such as angiograms, biopsies, and stent placements.
  • Nuclear Medicine: Uses small amounts of radioactive materials to diagnose and treat diseases, including PET scans and radioactive iodine therapy.
  • Ultrasound: Utilizes high-frequency sound waves to produce images of organs, tissues, and blood flow, commonly used in obstetrics and abdominal imaging.

    Facilities and Equipment

  • X-ray Machines: Standard and digital X-rays for imaging bones, lungs, and other internal structures.
  • CT (Computed Tomography) Scanners: Provides detailed cross-sectional images of the body for diagnosing a wide range of conditions.
  • MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) Scanners: Uses magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of organs and tissues, especially useful for brain, spine, and joint imaging.
