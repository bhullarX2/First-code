Emergency Department (Hameeda Hospital)

Overview

    Department Structure

  • Triage Area: Initial assessment area where patients are prioritized based on the severity of their condition.
  • Resuscitation Area: Equipped for critical cases requiring immediate life-saving interventions, such as cardiac arrest or severe trauma.
  • Acute Care: For patients with serious but not life-threatening conditions, such as fractures or severe infections.
  • Observation Unit: Monitors patients who need short-term care and assessment to determine if they require admission or can be discharged.

    Facilities and Equipment

  • Trauma Rooms: Fully equipped for handling severe injuries, including ventilators, defibrillators, and advanced monitoring systems.
  • Diagnostic Imaging: On-site access to X-rays, CT scans, and ultrasounds for quick diagnosis.
  • Laboratory Services: Rapid access to blood tests, toxicology, and other diagnostics to assist in immediate treatment decisions.
  • Pharmacy: On-site or nearby to provide necessary medications quickly, including emergency drugs and antidotes.
