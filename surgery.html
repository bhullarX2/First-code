Surgery Department (Hameeda Hospital)

Overview

    Department Structure

  • General Surgery: Handles a wide range of surgical procedures, including abdominal, breast, and thyroid surgeries.
  • Specialized Units: Includes units like Cardiothoracic Surgery, Neurosurgery, Orthopedic Surgery, and Plastic Surgery.
  • Surgical Teams: Comprises surgeons, anesthesiologists, surgical nurses, and support staff.

    Facilities and Equipment

  • Operating Theaters: Equipped with state-of-the-art technology for various types of surgeries.
  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU): For post-operative care and monitoring of critical patients.
  • Recovery Rooms: Where patients recover immediately after surgery under close supervision.

    Services Offered

  • Elective Surgeries: Planned surgeries that are not emergencies, such as joint replacements or cosmetic surgeries.
  • Emergency Surgeries: Urgent procedures required for life-threatening conditions, like appendectomies or trauma surgeries.
  • Minimally Invasive Surgeries: Includes laparoscopic and robotic surgeries that involve smaller incisions and faster recovery.
